Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Jimmie ("we," "us," "our") is an AI-powered concierge service operated by Taikun Inc. that provides restaurant, bar, and dining recommendations via SMS and messaging platforms. Our website is jimmie.ai.

What we collect

When you text Jimmie, we collect your phone number and the content of your messages. We use this information to provide personalized recommendations, remember your preferences across conversations, and improve our service.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily share it in conversation.

If you visit jimmie.ai, we may collect basic analytics data such as page views and referring URLs. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

How we use your information

Your phone number is used to identify your conversation thread so Jimmie can remember context and preferences across messages. Your message content is used to understand what you're looking for and provide relevant recommendations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to serve advertisements.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate Jimmie:

Twilio for SMS messaging, Anthropic's Claude API for generating responses, Stripe for processing voluntary tips, and Railway for hosting. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data that passes through their systems.

Tips and payments

If you choose to tip Jimmie, the payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information or payment details. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data.

Data retention

We retain your conversation history to provide a better experience over time. If you would like your data deleted, text STOP to Jimmie or contact us at the email below, and we will delete your conversation history and phone number from our system.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages from Jimmie at any time by texting STOP. You can resume by texting START. All conversations are user-initiated — we will not send unsolicited messages unless you have previously opted in to occasional updates.

Children's privacy

Jimmie is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request deletion of your data, contact us at collin.slattery@taikuninc.com.
